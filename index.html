A Voice for Every Child: Advocating Rights, Creating Change

A Voice for Every Child (AVEC) is a non-profit organization founded by Althea Enargan, Angela Sabater, and Dannica Vosotros to provide assistance to children in orphanages and victims of child abuse. AVEC promotes the holistic development of children all around the world through initiatives such as: tutoring programs, feeding programs, donation drives, and team-building activities for children residing in orphanages all around the Philippines. AVEC also aims to raise awareness of child abuse and its victims. Join us! and together we can create a supportive and inclusive space for children all around the world.
